# Phase 21: Outreach Response Templates

Use these templates to maintain truthfulness and ethical boundaries in all client communications.

---

## 1. Interested / Discovery Scheduling
"Thank you for your interest, [Name]! I'd love to schedule a 15-minute discovery call to understand your clinic’s specific workflow needs. We can also walk through the technical prerequisites for moving to a staging environment. Does next Tuesday work for you?"

---

## 2. Demo Requested
"Absolutely. We can perform a 30-minute walkthrough of the clinical and pharmacy modules. Please note that we strictly use synthetic demo data for these sessions to maintain total privacy. Would you like to see the 'Happy Path' clinical workflow or a deep dive into the security audit trail?"

---

## 3. Production Readiness Question
"That’s a great question. Gemini-HMS is currently in a verified 'Local Green' development baseline. Before we go live for production use, we require a client-funded staging validation phase to test at your specific scale. I can share our staging trigger plan if you’d like to see the steps required for a live pilot."

---

## 4. HIPAA / SOC2 Question
"We have architected the system specifically for HIPAA and SOC2 compliance, including httpOnly cookies, CSRF guards, and forensic auditing. However, we have not yet undergone formal third-party certification. That process usually happens during the cloud-staging phase. I’ve attached our current Security & Privacy Posture document for your technical team."

---

## 5. Real Patient Data Request
"To protect your patients' privacy, we strictly forbid the use of real patient data during the initial demonstration and local verification phases. We only process 100% synthetic data until a formal Business Associate Agreement (BAA) is signed and a secure, audited environment is provisioned."

---

## 6. Pricing / Staging Budget Question
"For Phase 21 (Staging), the primary cost is the cloud infrastructure itself (typically $50–$150/mo depending on the provider and scale). We discuss final production licensing after the staging validation is complete. Would you like to walk through the infrastructure requirements for a staging instance?"

---

## 7. Integration Request
"Integrating with legacy systems is definitely possible given our modular backend. We’ve parked custom integration requests for the technical discovery phase. Once we understand your current data structure, we can map out a sync strategy. Would you like to involve your IT lead in our next call?"

---

## 8. Not Interested
"Thank you for letting me know, [Name]. I’ve marked our records accordingly. If your needs change in the future, feel free to reach out. Best regards."

---

## 9. Opt-Out Acknowledgment
"Understood. You have been removed from our outreach list and we will not contact you again. Best regards."

---

## 10. Security Review Request
"We welcome technical scrutiny! I am escalating your request to our cybersecurity lead. In the meantime, I can provide our architecture summary which outlines our data isolation and CSRF protection strategies."
