# Client Call Agenda: Gemini-HMS Walkthrough

## 30-Minute Discovery & Demo (Condensed)
1. **Introductions** (2 Mins)
2. **Problem Discovery** (5 Mins) - *What is your biggest workflow bottleneck?*
3. **High-Level Functional Demo** (15 Mins) - *Triage to Dispense Loop.*
4. **Security & Deployment Overview** (5 Mins) - *Local Green Status & Cloud Needs.*
5. **Next Steps** (3 Mins) - *Staging Proposal.*

## 60-Minute Deep Dive (Technical/Decision-Maker)
1. **Stakeholder Introductions** (5 Mins)
2. **Operations Deep Dive** (10 Mins) - *Current tech stack and migration concerns.*
3. **End-to-End Workflow Demo** (25 Mins) - *Clinical, Lab, Pharmacy, and Audit.*
4. **Security & Compliance Q&A** (10 Mins) - *Audit logs, Encryption, and RBAC.*
5. **Deployment & Budget Roadmap** (7 Mins) - *GCP Staging requirements.*
6. **Wrap-up & Follow-up Deliverables** (3 Mins)

## Key Discovery Questions
- "How many clinics/branches do you currently manage?"
- "How do you currently handle handoffs between nurses and the pharmacy?"
- "What is your primary concern regarding data privacy and HIPAA?"
- "Do you have an existing GCP/AWS account for staging deployment?"
