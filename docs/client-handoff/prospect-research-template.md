# Prospect Research Template — Gemini-HMS

Use this template for every prospect before first outreach. Fill from public sources only. Do not scrape, buy, or extract private personal data.

## Template Fields

### 1. Organization Name
Record the full legal name of the clinic, lab, or hospital.

### 2. Website / Public Source
URL of the organization's public website or professional listing (Google Maps, clinic directory, LinkedIn company page).

### 3. Location
City, state/province, country. Note if multi-branch.

### 4. Type of Facility
- [ ] Small clinic chain (2–10 branches)
- [ ] Diagnostic / lab center
- [ ] Multi-branch outpatient facility
- [ ] Small hospital
- [ ] Other (specify)

### 5. Estimated Size
- [ ] 1–10 providers
- [ ] 11–50 providers
- [ ] 51–200 providers
- [ ] 200+ providers
- [ ] Unknown

Estimated monthly patient volume: ________

### 6. Current Workflow Clues
What does the prospect likely use today? Look for hints on website, job postings, or industry listings.
- [ ] Paper / spreadsheets
- [ ] Off-the-shelf EHR (name if known: ________)
- [ ] Custom / legacy system
- [ ] Separate lab system (LIS)
- [ ] Separate pharmacy system
- [ ] No patient portal
- [ ] Unknown

### 7. Likely Pain Points
Check all that apply based on facility type and size:
- [ ] Fragmented clinical/lab/pharmacy tools
- [ ] No role-based access control
- [ ] Manual lab result delivery to patients
- [ ] Pharmacy dispensing errors / no inventory tracking
- [ ] No audit trail for clinical changes
- [ ] High staff time on data entry
- [ ] Patients frequently call for results

### 8. Buyer / Contact Role
Record role-based contact (NOT private personal data):
- Clinic owner / CEO
- Hospital administrator
- IT Director / Head of Technology
- Operations Manager
- Medical Director
- Contact form available: Yes / No
- LinkedIn company page: Yes / No

### 9. Why Gemini-HMS May Fit
Brief rationale (1–3 sentences). Connect their likely pain points to Gemini-HMS capabilities:
- Multi-tenant RBAC for branch isolation?
- Integrated lab / pharmacy audit trail?
- Patient portal for self-service results?
- Clinical guardrails (prevent premature sign-off)?

### 10. Compliance / Budget Assumptions
- [ ] Likely to have HIPAA awareness
- [ ] Likely to have cloud-capable IT
- [ ] Likely to have budget for ~$50–150/mo staging
- [ ] Likely expects certification proof before commitment

### 11. Outreach Angle
What is the hook for the first message? Examples:
- "Saw your clinic network is expanding — our multi-branch platform may help unify workflows."
- "Noticed you operate a lab — we have an integrated LIS with staged validation."
- "Referral from [source]."

### 12. Risk / Fit Score
| Score | Meaning |
| :--- | :--- |
| **High Fit** | Multiple pain points align, likely budget and cloud awareness. |
| **Medium Fit** | Some alignment; needs qualification call. |
| **Low Fit** | Unclear need, no digital workflow pain visible, or poor-fit signal present (see disqualifying signals). |
| **Stop** | Disqualifying signal present (e.g., demands production-ready, HIPAA cert pre-demo, no budget). |

### 13. Next Action
- [ ] Send intro email / LinkedIn message
- [ ] Submit contact form inquiry
- [ ] Request warm introduction
- [ ] Wait for better timing
- [ ] Park — revisit in 3 months

## Privacy & Ethics Notes
- **Do not** store personal phone numbers, personal emails, or home addresses.
- **Do not** scrape data. Use only public business information.
- **Do not** share this template with prospects — internal use only.
- Review against GDPR / CCPA / local privacy law if operating outside the US.
