## Operational Rules
1. **No PHI**: Never include patient names or private identifiers in this KPI tracker.
2. **Ethics Threshold**: If `Complaints` > 0 or `Opt-Outs` > 25%, outreach MUST pause for a governance review.
3. **Quality over Volume**: A high `Average Fit Score` is more valuable than a high `Prospects Contacted` count.
