# Phase 21: Controlled First Outreach Launch Plan

## 1. Objective
Establish a high-fidelity, ethical, and truthful outreach system to identify the first 10–20 prospective clients for Gemini-HMS, focusing on those capable of funding a cloud staging environment.

## 2. Who to Contact First
- **Small-to-Medium Clinics**: Multi-specialty groups with 2–5 branches.
- **Private Diagnostic Labs**: Facilities needing digital result release and clinic integration.
- **Retail Pharmacy Groups**: Independent chains looking for a modern dispensing backend.
- **Healthcare IT Consultants**: Advisors looking for a security-hardened open-core baseline for their clients.

## 3. Who NOT to Contact
- **Individual solo practitioners**: High support overhead, low infrastructure budget.
- **Governmental Health Ministries**: Long sales cycles, complex procurement beyond current scope.
- **Clients demanding immediate real-data testing**: Security/legal boundary violation.
- **Clients requiring SOC2/HIPAA certification pre-demo**: We are "Designed for" but not yet "Certified."

## 4. Ideal First 10–20 Prospect Criteria
- Uses an outdated or manual (paper-based) workflow.
- Operates at least 2 physical locations.
- Has a dedicated IT contact or technically literate owner.
- Explicitly willing to invest in their own cloud infrastructure ($50–$150/mo staging).

## 5. Ethical Research Rules
- **Public Sources Only**: LinkedIn Company Pages, Hospital Directories, Official Websites.
- **Role-Based Contact**: Target official role inboxes (e.g., info@, it@, admin@) or contact forms.
- **No Private Harvesting**: Strictly forbidden to scrape personal cell numbers or private personal emails.
- **No Deception**: Clearly identify as representatives of the Gemini-HMS project.
- **Respect Opt-Out**: If a prospect says "Not interested" or "Stop," flag them in the tracker immediately and never contact again.

## 6. Outreach Sequence
1.  **Initial Contact**: Personalized message identifying a specific pain point (e.g., "manual lab result release").
2.  **Qualification**: 15-minute discovery call to confirm infrastructure readiness.
3.  **Local Demo**: 30-minute walkthrough using **synthetic data only**.
4.  **Staging Proposal**: Transition to Phase 21 client-funded staging.

## 7. Demo Scheduling Flow
- Use a standard scheduling link (e.g., Calendly) or direct manual coordination.
- **Prerequisite**: Send the `sales-ready-baseline-status.md` summary before the demo.

## 8. Qualification Gate (Pre-Demo)
Before a live demo is scheduled, the prospect must confirm:
- They understand the system is currently "Local Green" (Development Baseline).
- They agree to see **synthetic data only**.

## 9. Staging Trigger Gate (Post-Demo)
To move beyond the demo, the prospect must commit to the `phase-21-staging-trigger-plan.md` requirements.

## 10. Stop Rules
- **PHI Request**: If the client demands to upload real patient data before a BAA/Staging setup, stop and explain the security policy.
- **Budget Rejection**: If the client is unwilling to fund a $50/mo staging environment, disqualify as a Phase 21 candidate.

## 11. Risks and Mitigations
- **Reputational Risk**: Mitigated by extreme truthfulness regarding certification status.
- **Data Leak**: Mitigated by strict local-only execution and hardened demo scripts.
