# Phase 21: Weekly Outreach Review Checklist

Use this checklist every Friday to monitor the health and ethics of the current outreach cycle.

## 1. Volume & Quality Review
- [ ] **Research Integrity**: Verify all 10–20 prospects in the tracker originated from public organization-level sources.
- [ ] **No Private Harvesting**: Confirm zero personal cell numbers or private emails were added.
- [ ] **Personalization Check**: Randomly audit 3 sent messages to ensure placeholders were correctly filled and rules followed.

## 2. Response & Engagement
- [ ] **Classification Audit**: Verify all replies were correctly categorized using the `phase-21-response-classification-matrix.md`.
- [ ] **Template Fidelity**: Confirm sent responses adhered to the `phase-21-response-templates.md` without overclaiming.
- [ ] **Demo Status**: Identify all "Demo Requested" leads and confirm `phase-21-demo-request-triage-checklist.md` was completed.

## 3. Privacy & Ethics Monitor
- [ ] **Opt-Out Integrity**: Confirm all "Stop" or "Not interested" requests were marked STOP and all follow-ups were cancelled.
- [ ] **No-Follow-up Check**: Verify zero contact was made with any prospect marked as Opt-Out in previous weeks.
- [ ] **Complaint Audit**: Review any negative feedback regarding outreach frequency or style.

## 4. Escalation & Safety
- [ ] **PHI/Security Review**: Review any technical or compliance questions escalated during the week.
- [ ] **Incident Check**: Confirm no operator errors occurred (e.g., accidental PHI exposure during demo).

## 5. Decision Rules
- [ ] **Continue**: Proceed with next batch if opt-out rate is below 20% and no complaints.
- [ ] **Adjust**: Modify message variants if positive reply rate is below 5%.
- [ ] **Pause**: STOP all outreach immediately if any "Blocking" safety incident occurs or if legal/security concerns are raised.
