3. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
======================================================================

The strongest current problem is not one isolated page. It is portal coherence.

The codebase currently has four recurring integrity failures:

1. Users can see or launch actions that do not belong to their role or portal.
2. Users do not see some pages that actually do belong to them.
3. Some dashboards point into hidden or dead destinations.
4. Some portal screens look operational while still being structurally sparse, mock-driven, or visually weak.

This means the project is not mainly blocked by raw crashes in this lane. It is blocked by misleading navigation truth, weak role ownership boundaries, and dashboard pages that do not reliably express what the portal actually owns.
